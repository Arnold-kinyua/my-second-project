Akan name genarator

Description

In this weeks independent project we are supposed to get the users birthday and make the application calculate the day of the week they were born in reference to their Akan name.

The akan day names are written as follows:

MALE

  • Sunday: Kwasi
  • Monday: Kwadwo
  • Tuesday: Kwabena
  • Wednesday: Kwaku
  • Thursday: Yaw
  • Friday: Kofi
  • Saturday: Kwame

FEMALE

  • Sunday: Akosua
  • Monday: Adwoa
  • Tuesday: Abenaa
  • Wednesday: Akua
  • Thursday: Yaa
  • Friday: Afua
  • Saturday: Ama
